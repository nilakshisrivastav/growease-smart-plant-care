# 🌱 GrowEase – Smart Plant Care Companion

GrowEase is a smart plant care mobile application designed to help plant lovers manage and care for their plants effectively. The app provides detailed plant care information, personalized reminders for watering and fertilizing, and a plant shopping feature to help users choose plants suitable for different seasons.

## 📱 Objective

The objective of GrowEase is to simplify plant care by providing users with:

- Easy access to plant care information
- Timely reminders for plant maintenance
- A convenient platform to explore and buy seasonal plants

This helps users maintain healthy plants and enhances their gardening experience.

## ✨ Features

- 🌿 **Plant Information**
  - View detailed information about different plants
  - Learn plant benefits and care instructions

- ☀️ **Plant Care Guidance**
  - Sunlight requirements
  - Watering frequency
  - Soil type recommendations
  - Expert plant care tips

- ⏰ **Smart Reminders**
  - Set reminders for watering and fertilizing
  - Toggle reminders on/off
  - Full-screen in-app notifications

- 🛒 **Plant Shopping**
  - Explore plants suitable for different seasons
  - Select and purchase multiple plants

- 🔔 **Notification Control**
  - Enable or disable reminders anytime
  - Cancel notifications when needed
